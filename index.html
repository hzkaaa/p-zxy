꧁ XINYU ZHANG ꧂

I am a postgraduate student in Computer Arts and my undergraduate degree is in Digital Media Arts.

I am interested in the intersection of interaction design and performance studies. Currently, the stage effect of the same space and performer can change depending on the differences in audience. Stage directors often try to erase those differences with technology and formulate beauty. However, I believe them to be the driving factor of an evolving stage, vitalising beauty.

My main artworks take the form of interactive or immersive artworks, such as interactive installations, audio visual and interactive performances. I will continue to explore the ecology of performance, trying to use interactive technologies to break the creative limits of traditional performance, to enhance the dialogue between performer and audience, and to build a more vivid performance.
